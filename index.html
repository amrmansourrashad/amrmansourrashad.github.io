About Me

I am a Roads Construction Engineer at the Ministry of Transport (Egypt), working at the Central Administration for Roads Construction and Maintenance. With a strong academic background including a Master's degree in Highway and Airport Engineering from Minia University, I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. at Aswan University.

My research focuses on optimized numerical models of slope stability and road embankment analysis. I have published peer-reviewed papers on slope stability using limit equilibrium methods and soil nailing techniques. Beyond research, I founded "Be Best Engineering," a popular blog that has reached 150,000 views, sharing knowledge and resources with the civil engineering community.

I combine theoretical expertise with practical experience in highway design, traffic engineering, and project management, utilizing advanced software tools including Civil 3D, Plaxis 2D, GeoStudio, and MATLAB.
